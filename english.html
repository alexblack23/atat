Cutting diamond wheels are tools for cutting extremely durable materials. Usually in such cases we are talking about stone, brick, slate, ceramic tile, as well as glass. Diamond cutting wheels should be selected according to the material for which they are intended to be cut. It cannot be said that the cut quality with an inappropriate circle will be much worse, but it is definitely worth noting that it will dull much faster and will not be suitable for long-term operation. Diamond cutting wheels are divided into several types, each of it has a specific configuration and is used for dry or wet cutting. Wet cuts are used where there is an increased need for cooling the surface of the parts, as well as the working tool itself. For dry cutting is quite suitable segment circle, which will be cooled due to its shape. But for a wet one you can take a regular disk. Of course, you shouldn’t forget that it’s possible to work with diamond cutting circles continuously for only 1-2 minutes, since longer use will lead to the fact that diamonds become useless. Cutting diamond wheels at reasonable prices you can purchase from us. We offer you to buy the following types circles: regular, segmental, segmental for deep cuts, as well as turbo and turbo wave. Each of them is manufactured according to high standards of production using quality materials. Cutting diamond wheels from the company "ATT" are a reliable tool for the realization of your most diverse ideas related to the processing of high-strength materials.
Grinding (flap) wheels are used by Ukrainians in order to get rid of the rust and scale present on the surface of various parts, as well as products. These circles are used directly and for the grinding itself, which helps to achieve the required degree of roughness. If we talk about the design of grinding (flap) wheels, then it fully corresponds to its name, and consists of petals. They, in turn. overlapped in a circle. The circles get their polishing qualities due to the use of crumb from zirconium electro corundum, which sits on epoxy glue. Grinding (flap) circles differ in the granularity of this very crumb, and the larger it is in size, the rougher the surface to be treated. This type of circles is used for delicate removal of varnish and paint, processing of uneven edges of the metal after the cutting process, the elimination of burrs. But to give smoothness and glossy shine this tool, alas, will not work, but the cloth circles for polishing together with special abrasive pastes will definitely be the right decision. To work with the grinder fit circles of small diameter, but for use in industry choose a polishing tool of a sufficiently large diameter.
